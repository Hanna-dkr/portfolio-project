UX Case Study: A web app designed to help workaholics find balance

Why workaholics and why a web app?

Workaholics struggle with work life balance, which impacts their well being. Workahoilism has always been of interest to me because I know a lot of people who self-identify as workaholics. IO have observed them struggle to disengage from work and maintain balance in their lives. I have heard how this negatively affects their health, wellbeing and relationships. I wanted to understand more about their pain points to see if there was a tech solution that could help them.

The user research done for this project showed that this would be more appropriate for workaholics. The findings of this research are detailed later on in the case study.
